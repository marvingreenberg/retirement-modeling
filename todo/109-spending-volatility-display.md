# 109. Spending Volatility Display & MC Chart Colors

## Spending Volatility

Show spending variation across strategies to make the fixed-dollar vs fixed-% vs guardrails tradeoff visible.

### Monte Carlo: Spending fan chart
- Add spending percentile bands (p5/p50/p95) to the Spending tab when MC results are available
- Similar to the existing balance fan chart
- Fixed-dollar: tight bands. Fixed-%: wide bands. Guardrails: in between.

### Single Run: Compare table metric
- Add "Spending Range" or similar to the compare summary (e.g., min–max annual spending, or std dev)
- Helps quantify lifestyle stability at a glance

## MC Chart Colors

The Monte Carlo fan chart colors are not very visible in dark mode. Evaluate and adjust the palette for better contrast on dark backgrounds.
